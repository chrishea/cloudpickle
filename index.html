Mission

The Club's purpose is to promote and facilitate the sport of pickleball in Cloudcroft, New Mexico. It aims to foster community engagement, improve members' skills, and provide opportunities for social and competitive play. To work with the Village of Cloudcroft to obtain funding through available means. To improve our relationship with the community and work with both to attract tourists to our village. To assist, when available, other organizations with their fundraisers when pertaining to Pickleball. To work with our youth to offer them another healthy activity.

Membership

Membership is open to all individuals interested in pickleball, regardless of age, gender, or skill level. Members have the right to participate in all Club activities, vote on Club matters, and run for office. Members must adhere to the Club's Code of Conduct and contribute to a positive and respectful environment. Membership may be revoked at any time by a majority vote of the Board of Directors.
